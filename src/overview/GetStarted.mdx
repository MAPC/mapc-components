## CONTRIBUTING

The digital project portfolio at MAPC is always evolving! The Digital Services team works in a cyclical workflow, when tasked with developing new projects look out for components that could be drawn out as a standalone component to be reused; continuously contributing new useful components to MAPC Components for reuse in future projects!

To contribute a new component, please reference the steps in [Contributing](?path=/docs/misc-contributing--docs)
